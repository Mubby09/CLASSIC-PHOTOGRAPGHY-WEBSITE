Company’s Profile

Classic Photography is a one-stop media house for top quality images and related photography services including video shoots, advert shoot and photoshoots, canvass framing, special photo books, glazed photo frames, adverts and media. Our exclusively high quality and cost-effective service delivery explain why we remain attractive and preferred to clients. Our excellent customer service and competitive prices also ensure that we stand out amongst competitors. This among other reasons has made Classic Photography the preferred media hub for many. We have been able to achieve this through quality service delivery and by training and retraining our best hands-on required skills and professionalism. At Classic Photography, we leave long-lasting memories with our clients by constantly surpassing expectations. We hold our existing customers in high esteem while we constantly watch out for new clients. To showcase a piece of our excellent driven service to new clients, we go out of our ways to offer highly discounted photoshoot sessions as incentives. Our fast-growing client base includes individuals, corporate bodies, students, schools, professional event planners and government at all levels: LIRS, Lagos state ministry of Arts and Tourism, Laralec Construction Company, Japan International Corporation Agency, Lagos State Electricity Board, CACOVID, JIO Foto, Dexter Fotos Limited, Ogun State Ministry of Finance and Treasure House of God Church, Abeokuta and Jaiyeola Hospital in Lagos. And sincerely we can’t wait to have you join our list of happy and satisfied customers.
