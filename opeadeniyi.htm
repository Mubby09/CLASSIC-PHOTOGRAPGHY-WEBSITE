About Me

Adeniyi Opeoluwa is a professional photographer based in Lagos, Nigeria, an event planner and an entrepreneur. Ope is the Creative Director of Opeclassic Global Services, the parent company of Classic Photography, an organization that specializes in event coverage, photo-framing and media consultancy. Adeniyi Opeoluwa is the official and personal Photographer of the former Governor of Lagos State, Mr Akinwunmi Ambode, and that provided him with the ample opportunity to document and showcase the developmental strides of the Ambode administration to the good people of Lagos. Ope, as he is fondly called is the author of “From vision to Execution”, a pictorial compendium of some selected achievements of the Governor Ambode led administration. Opeoluwa is a creative photographer with exceptional skills in producing premium-quality photos in the studio and outdoor settings. One of the qualities that set Opeoluwa apart and endear him to the heart of clients is his depth of creativity and extraordinary ability to capture and create mind-blowing images. He is equally very versed in other facets of photography such as scheduling, sets designing and photo editing. Earlier in his journey, Ope joined Fototek as an apprentice in 2001, however, it didn’t take long before his attention to details and exceptional qualities were noticed by the management. Consequently, he was transferred to the Bayelsa branch of the company in 2002, where he served as the studio manager. Before his recent assignment as the personal photographer to the former Governor Akinwunmi Ambode, Opeoluwa served as the studio manager for Photo Fast Limited’s branch in Bayelsa State, before he was transferred to the Port Harcourt branch. Opeoluwa is a graduate of Educational Psychology from the University of Lagos, UNILAG. He is married to Omowunmi and they are blessed with children.
